Who We Are

Two Pillars.
One Vision
For Africa.

GAMA TT is an innovation-led company operating at the intersection of sustainable transportation and transformative urban development. We believe Africa's future must be built by Africans — designed for its terrain, its people, and its ambition.

Through our Automotive division, we are developing the VAYO electric vehicle platform to decarbonize transport across Rwanda and beyond. Through our Architecture & Development division, we are realizing iNTORE Estate — a landmark mixed-use development in Bugesera.

MUTARA

A district rooted in nature and sustainability — drawing inspiration from Rwanda's lush northern highlands. MUTARA will feature eco-residential zones and community parks designed to bring residents into harmony with the natural landscape.

ResidentialSustainability
District 02 — KINTSUGI
02
District 02
KINTSUGI

Named after the Japanese art of repairing with gold — KINTSUGI is a district that celebrates heritage, beauty, and resilience. Designed as a cultural and artisan hub, it will house galleries, creative studios, boutique hospitality, and high-end mixed-use spaces.

RUREMA

IJABIRO
